About Me

I am a 30-year-old Postdoctoral Researcher at IMEDEA UIB-CSIC, Mallorca (Spain), with extensive experience in large-scale ocean circulation, particularly in assessing and reconstructing oceanic vertical dynamics using observation-based products.

Currently, I work within the ESA 4DMED-SEA project gaining experience in Lagrangian methodologies applied to altimetry-based surface and ocean interior velocities, focusing on tracking transport fronts and coherent eddies in the Mediterranean Sea.
