About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Mediterranean Institute of Advanced Studies (IMEDEA) in Mallorca, Spain. My research focuses on vertical ocean dynamics, Ekman layer processes, and observation-based velocity reconstructions.

Research Interests

  • Vertical ocean circulation and Ekman pumping
  • Lagrangian transport and mixing in the ocean
  • Observation-based velocity reconstruction and vorticity balance
