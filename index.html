About Me

I am a 29-year-old Postoctoral Researcher in IMEDEA UIB-CSIC, Mallorca (Spain), with vast experience in large-scale ocean circulation, assessing oceanic vertical dynamics and reconstructing them using observation-based products. Currently, working in the ESA 4DMED-SEA project under the supervision of Dr. Ismael Hernández-Carrasco and Dr. Ananda Pascual I am gaining experience in Lagrangian methodologies applied to altimetry-based surface and ocean interior velocities, focusing on tracking transport fronts and coherent eddies in the Mediterranean Sea.

Research Interests

  • Large-scale Vertical Ocean Interior Circulation and Ekman Pumping
  • Lagrangian Transport and Mesoscale Coherent Structures
  • Observation-based Reconstruction of Physical Variables
